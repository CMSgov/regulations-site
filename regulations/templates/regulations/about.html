How It Works

Automated and editorial update processes

Regulations

This tool displays regulation text from eCFR, automatically pulled once a day to maintain accuracy.

The Office of the Federal Register updates eCFR daily, and it incorporates new amendments about two business days after their effective date.

Note: eCFR and this tool are not official legal editions of the CFR. See eCFR User Information for how to check against official materials (annual editions and Federal Register rules).

About the eCFR data source

Federal Register Rules

The lists of rules (proposed and final amendments to regulations) are pulled automatically from the Office of the Federal Register.

These lists are fetched every time you load a page that includes them, so they are always up-to-date.

About the Federal Register data source

Links to Additional Resources

Resource links are curated and updated by a Medicaid subject matter expert on our team.

For example, when Medicaid.gov publishes a new State Medicaid Director Letter, we add a link in the sidebar of each relevant subpart.
